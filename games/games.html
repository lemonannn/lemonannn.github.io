Drawn Together, Pulled Apart

Join up with a partner, use your magnetic forces to attract, repel, and interact with each other and with the environment to solve clever, cooperative puzzles.
Drawn Together, Pulled Apart was created by myself and four other developers in a time span of about six weeks using Unity and C#. My role focused mostly on creating and implementing the UI elements, designing levels, and establishing interesting ways for the magnets to work with each other.
